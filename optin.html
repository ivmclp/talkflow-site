SMS Opt‑In Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-09

By submitting your phone number on our website or lead forms, you consent to receive SMS messages from TalkFlow about your real estate inquiry. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

How users opt in

  • Users provide their phone number through a web form requesting information about a property or service, and click submit.
  • The form clearly states that by submitting, the user agrees to receive SMS messages related to their inquiry.

What we send

  • Timely responses to the user’s inquiry (e.g., availability, scheduling a tour, follow‑ups).
  • No promotional blasts unrelated to the original inquiry.

Opt‑out & help

  • Reply STOP to opt out of future messages at any time.
  • Reply HELP for assistance or email founder@talkflow.ai.

Privacy

We only use the information you provide to respond to your inquiry. See our simple privacy summary at privacy.html.
